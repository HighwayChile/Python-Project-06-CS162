This is Parsin' Lists, a sorting visualizer by Richard Sapp

Use the slider bar to choose any number of bars between 2 and 42, 
then hit "JUMBLE" to generate a new list, or leave it at the default
value of 20.

Set speed with dropdown menu.

choose your sort method by clicking on the corresponding button

Bubble sort on slow is not for the faint of heart.

requires Tkinter, ttk, PhotoImage, and pytest.
